## Training Videos

### Video 1: Platform Introduction (5 minutes)

#### Script
```
[Opening Scene: Benefits Assistant logo and welcome screen]

Narrator: "Welcome to the Benefits Assistant training series. I'm [Name], and I'll be guiding you through this powerful AI-powered benefits platform.

[Screen shows main dashboard]

The Benefits Assistant is designed to help employees understand and manage their benefits through intelligent conversations with an AI assistant. Let's start with the basics.

[Screen shows login page]

First, you'll access the platform through your company's single sign-on system. Once logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard.

[Screen shows chat interface]

The main feature is our chat interface. You can ask questions about your benefits in natural language, just like talking to a human benefits specialist.

[Screen shows example conversation]

For example, you can ask 'What's covered under my health insurance?' or 'How much would I pay for a dental cleaning?' The AI will provide detailed, personalized answers based on your specific plan.

[Screen shows document upload feature]

You can also upload documents like benefit summaries or insurance cards for analysis. The AI can extract key information and answer questions about the documents.

[Screen shows conversation history]

All your conversations are saved, so you can refer back to them anytime. You can also export your chat history for your records.

[Closing scene with contact information]

That's the basics! In our next video, we'll dive deeper into specific features and advanced capabilities. Thanks for watching!"
```

#### Key Points Covered
- Platform overview and purpose
- Login and navigation
- Basic chat functionality
- Document upload capability
- Conversation history

#### Script
```

```

First, let's access the admin dashboard. You'll need administrator credentials to access these features.

As an administrator, you have powerful tools to ensure the platform meets your organization's needs and provides value to your employees."

## Hands-on Exercises

### Exercise 1: Basic Chat Interaction

#### Objective
Learn to use the chat interface effectively

#### Steps
1. Log into the Benefits Assistant platform
2. Start a new conversation
3. Ask the following questions:
   - "What's covered under my health insurance?"
   - "How much would I pay for a dental cleaning?"
   - "Can you help me find a doctor in my network?"
4. Upload a benefit summary document
5. Ask questions about the uploaded document
6. Export your conversation history

#### Expected Outcome
Users should be comfortable with basic chat functionality and document upload

### Exercise 2: Advanced Features

#### Objective
Explore advanced platform features

#### Steps
1. Use the cost calculator to compare plan options
2. Search for providers in your area
3. Use the benefits comparison tool
4. Customize your notification preferences
5. Explore the conversation history
6. Test the mobile interface

#### Expected Outcome
Users should understand all available features and how to use them effectively

### Exercise 3: Admin Functions

#### Objective
Learn administrative tasks and monitoring

#### Steps
1. Access the admin dashboard
2. Add a new user account
3. Review analytics and reports
4. Check cost monitoring
5. Update company configuration
6. Review system health metrics

#### Expected Outcome
Administrators should be able to manage the platform effectively

## Assessment Materials

### Quiz 1: Basic Platform Knowledge

#### Questions
1. What is the main purpose of the Benefits Assistant platform?
2. How do you start a new conversation?
3. What types of documents can you upload?
4. How do you find providers in your network?
5. Where can you view your conversation history?

#### Answer Key
1. To help employees understand and manage their benefits through AI-powered chat
2. Type a question in the chat box and press Enter
3. PDF, DOC, DOCX, and image files
4. Use the provider search feature
5. In the conversation history section

### Quiz 2: Advanced Features

#### Questions
1. How does the cost calculator help users?
2. What is the benefits comparison tool used for?
3. How can you customize notifications?
4. What information does the AI provide about uploaded documents?
5. How do you export conversation history?

#### Answer Key
1. It helps calculate out-of-pocket costs and compare plan options
2. To compare different benefit plans side-by-side
3. Through the notification settings in your profile
4. It can extract key information and answer questions about the content
5. Click the export button in the conversation history section

### Quiz 3: Administrative Functions

#### Questions
1. What are the different user roles available?
2. How do you add a new user?

5. What is the purpose of company configuration?

#### Answer Key
1. Employee, Company Admin, Super Admin
