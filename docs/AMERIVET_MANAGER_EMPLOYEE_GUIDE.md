3. Review the detailed comparison table

**Comparison includes**:
- Monthly premiums
- Deductibles
- Co-pays and co-insurance
- Out-of-pocket maximums
- Network providers
- Coverage details

**What it does**: Upload and analyze your benefit documents

**How to use**:
1. Click "Upload Document" button
2. Select your file (PDF, Word, or image)

4. Click "Upload"

**Supported files**:

- **Solutions**:
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Refresh the page
  3. Try asking a simpler question

### **Error Messages**

#### **"Session Expired"**
- **What it means**: You've been logged out automatically
- **What to do**: Simply log in again with your password

#### **"Rate Limit Exceeded"**
- **What it means**: You're making too many requests too quickly
- **What to do**: Wait a few minutes and try again

#### **"File Too Large"**
- **What it means**: Your document is bigger than 50MB
- **What to do**: Compress the file or split it into smaller parts

#### **"Invalid File Type"**
- **What it means**: Your file format isn't supported
- **What to do**: Convert to PDF, Word, or text format
